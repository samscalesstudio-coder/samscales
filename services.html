The Menu

Cuts, fades, beards
& the hot towel.

We call them what they are — cuts, fades, beards, shaves, line-ups and hot towels. No "looks", no "vibes". Just precise work, finished by eye, every chair, every time.

01

Signature Cut

A full scissor or clipper cut, shaped to your head and how you wear it. Two-minute consultation, mirror checks, clean finish.

02

Skin Fade

A fade that disappears into the skin — high, mid or low. Blended by hand until there isn't a line to find.

03

Beard Sculpt & Line-up

Beard shaped, cheek and neck lines set sharp. Trimmed to your face, not a template.

04

Hot-Towel Shave

Traditional wet shave with a single-use blade and a hot towel. Close, calm, and finished with balm.

05

Cut + Beard Combo

The full reset — signature cut paired with a beard sculpt and line-up. Everything sharp at once.

06

Kids' Cut (5+)

A calm cut for younger clients, age five and up, with a parent on the premises. Patient hands, quick chair.

07

Manicure

Nails shaped and cleaned, cuticles tidied, hands left sharp. Add colour if you want it.

08

Pedicure

Feet soaked, nails trimmed and shaped, hard skin smoothed away. Clean and comfortable.

09

Facial

A proper clean-up for the skin — cleanse, scrub, steam and finish. Walk out fresh.

10

Hair Colour & Chemical

Colour, global tint and chemical treatments — done with care and the right products for your hair.

The Samscales Standard

Five movements.
One signature.

Whether you sit with a senior barber or the newest hire, the service runs the same five movements — so the Samscales experience is consistent, every chair, every time.

I

The Greeting

Stand to greet. Eye contact, name where we know it, water or coffee within thirty seconds.

II

The Consultation

Two minutes minimum. Length, line and finish agreed before the first guard goes on.

III

The Cut

Clean tools, phone away, mirror checks at three intervals. We don't rush the line.

IV

The Finish

Hot towel where it's included, neck brushed clean, product only with consent.

V

The Farewell

Walk to the till, confirm the next appointment, thanked by name.
